global foreskin.live community rules

These rules apply to all user-generated content posted/submitted to/created using Foreskin.live's tools, servers, and resources.

Last revised March 20, 2021 at 3:24pm EST - Archives of the rules


  1. Do not engage against targetted harrassment or encourage targetted harrassment towards groups based on (including, but not limited to) race, sexual orientation, gender, personal interests, abilities, and age.
  2. Do not knowingly post, link, or otherwise direct people towards content that is illegal in the United States or images containing nudity of any kind (including illustrations).
  3. Do not use any service to publicize anybody's personal information and/or content the person isn't comfortable (otherwise known as "doxxing"), including public figures.
  4. Do not ruin or attempt to ruin other people's work (an example is greifing something in the Minecraft server) without expressed permission from the original creator.
  5. Do not use any of our services to spam or advertise. You can advertise in the Discord server, however, do not join to exclusively do that.

Did someone violate a rule? Report it here.

Ban appeals are available as well, but may not be available depending the severity of the violation.
Because of this, you will only be given a ban link if we feel you deserve a second chance.
